5-Whys Root Cause

  1. Why was HF in the wrong cabinet? — Placed during shift turnover without bin verification.
  2. Why no verification? — Outgoing tech skipped barcode scan due to time pressure.
  3. Why time pressure? — Late WIP surge from upstream process.
  4. Why surge caused skipping? — Handover checklist lacked “critical chem verify” step.
  5. Why checklist gap? — Procedure revision didn’t reflect new barcode workflow.
